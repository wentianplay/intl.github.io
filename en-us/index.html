Worldview Setting

Core Setting

In 8956 AD, the "Blue Star Empire" accidentally crashes into the TianBIN continent. After humans establish Tiansi City, they face invasions from the妖族 (Yao Tribe/Demon Beast Tribe) and魔族 (Mo Tribe/Demon Tribe). Ancestral Emperor Su Tianbin leads the Mystics to repel the invaders, but factional strife triggers the cataclysmic event "Heaven's Annihilation of All Life." The player's mission is to explore the truth behind the conflict between the Surface and Hidden Worlds and uncover the mystery behind Ancestral Emperor Su Tianbin's destruction of the world.

Mystic-Tech Dual System

Players can choose to become a Spiritual Power Master (Mystic), a Mechanical Controller (Techist), or a Dual-Cultivation Cross-Border Practitioner. The cultivation system includes spiritual energy cultivation, technique learning, alchemy and artifact forging, and transcending tribulations to ascend. The tech system includes mecha assembly, AI control, genetic modification, and interstellar technology. The dual-cultivation system supports cross-boundary fusion gameplay.

Multiple Races

The three major races - Humans, Yao Tribe (Demon Beasts), and Mo Tribe (Demons) - each have unique skills. Players can freely choose their race, join different factions, participate in territorial disputes, resource allocation, and other political and economic activities, influencing the fate of the entire TianBIN continent.

Game Features

Sandbox Exploration

Seamless hexagon-based vast map covering snow mountains/deserts/oases and other ecologies, containing hidden treasures and random encounters

Dynamic Combat

Side-scrolling 2.5D top-down real-time combat, emphasizing combo chains, positioning, and team coordination

Territory Capture

Compete for territories on the hexagonal strategy map, triggering PvP upon capture, influencing the world's structure

Social & Construction

Form expedition teams to conquer dungeons, establish factions to influence the world's political and economic landscape

Development History

2017

Project Launch (March 2017)

"TianBIN" was first initiated by Su Tianbin (Li Wenfei), founder of Wentianplay Network Technology, establishing the core worldview of Mystic-Tech Dualism.

2024

Technical Breakthrough (March 2024)

Completed core Unity engine development, realized side-scrolling 2.5D sandbox map and dual-cultivation system. Surpassed 560,000 players across the year.

2025

Project Pause (Early 2025)

Project temporarily halted due to technical refactoring needs. Team focused on AI large models and art style upgrades.

2028

Development Restart (March 2028)

Project restarted, utilizing self-developed AI large models to enhance interactive experience, optimizing anime-inspired art style.
